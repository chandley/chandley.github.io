Chess Pie

May 1, 2025

mix

  • 1 cup packed brown sugar
  • ½ cup granulated sugar
  • 1T flour

beat in

  • 2 eggs
  • 2T milk
  • 1t vanilla
  • ½ cup melted butter until thick & light

fold in

  • 1C chopped walnuts or pecans

put in 20cm shortcrust pie shell

bake 190c for 40-50 minutes
