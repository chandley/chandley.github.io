* 1 1/2 cup cooked or canned pumpkin
* 1/2 cup sugar
* 2 T flour
* 1/4 t nutmeg
* 1/4 t ginger
* 1/2 t salt
* 1/4 cup corn syrup
* 1 1/2 cup milk (or equal mix evaporated milk and water)
* 2 eggs well beaten
* 3T sugar
* 1t vanilla essence
set aside

Mix then cook over low heat
* 3 egg yolks
* 2-3T tapioca
* 2T sugar
* 1/4 cup milk

When spoon is coated and mixture is bubbling dully, add egg white
