mix
* 1 cup packed brown sugar
* ½ cup granulated sugar
* 1T flour

beat in
* 2 eggs
* 2 T milk
* 1 t vanilla
* ½ cup melted butter
  until thick & light

fold in
* 1 cup chopped walnuts or pecans

put in 20cm shortcrust pie shell

bake 190c for 40-50 minutes
