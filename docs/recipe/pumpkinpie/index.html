Pumpkin Pie (Mother recipe)

May 4, 2025

heat over medium heat for 10 minutes

  • 1 ½ cup cooked or canned pumpkin
  • ½ cup sugar
  • 2 T flour
  • ¼ t nutmeg
  • ¼ t ginger
  • ½ t salt
  • ¼ cup corn syrup
  • 1 ½ cup milk (or equal mix evaporated milk and water)
  • 2 eggs well beaten
  • 3T sugar
  • 1t vanilla essence set aside

Mix then cook over low heat

  • 3 egg yolks
  • 2-3T tapioca
  • 2T sugar
  • ¼ cup milk

When spoon is coated and mixture is bubbling dully, add egg white
