Chess Pie

mix

  • 1 cup packed brown sugar
  • ½ cup granulated sugar
  • 1T flour

beat in

  • 2 eggs
  • 2T milk
  • 1t vanilla
  • ½ cup melted butter until thick & light

fold in

  • 1C chopped walnuts or pecans

put in 20cm shortcrust pie shell

bake 190c for 40-50 minutes

Eggnog

beat

  • 6 egg yolks
  • 4oz icing sugar
  • ½ pint milk
  • ½ bottle brandy
  • 2oz rum

whip and fold in

  • 1pt double cream

beat to soft peak and fold in

  • 6 egg whites

serve with sprinkling of nutmeg
